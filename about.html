who are you?

my name is ian doherty, and my work mostly concerns the intersections of computers, audio, and music. i'm particularly interested in musical systems (e.g., daws, plugins), dsp related stuff, and coding all of the above.

i am a class of 2025 graduate of the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign's B.S. in Computer Science + Music program. there, i studied under Dr. Kerry Hagan, Dr. Paris Smaragdis, and Dr. Eli Fieldsteel. my program taught us about Pure Data, dsp, and electroacoustic composition in the context of a robust computer science program. so most of my skills involve working with these things through code.

in August 2025, i plan to begin my studies for McGill University's M.A. in Music Technology program in Montreal, Canada.

i also do a lot of music production and performance in my spare time. i have produced hip hop beats under the name IDES, recorded and engineered my friends' bands, and performed rock/folk music as myself and my band, spingo.
